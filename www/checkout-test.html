Explanatory text

The recommended daily* calorie intake for an average man who wishes to maintain his weight is 2500 calories. A positive 'calories exceeded' number indicates the amount of calories you over-ate for a given day. A negative number indicates that you have ate less than the recommended daily calorie intake. Men who are trying to lose weight should stick to a daily intake of 1800 calories. Each meal should contain 450 calories and snacks should total 450 calories a day.

Note * clicking on Consumption Report indicates calorie intake for a given day. If you click twice during a session, the app will assume you wish to calculate your intake for two days.
