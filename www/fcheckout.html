Less recommended daily calorie intake for a woman (2000 calories):

Calories Exceeded:

Explanatory text

The recommended daily* calorie intake for an average woman who wishes to maintain her weight is 2000 calories. A positive calories exceeded number indicates the amount of calories you have overeat for a given day. A negative number indicates that you have eaten less than the recommended daily calorie intake. Women who are trying to lose weight should stick to a daily intake of 1200 calories a day. This includes three meals of about 300 calories each and a total of 300 calories in snacks.

Note * clicking on Consumption Report indicates calorie intake for a given day. If you click twice during a session, the app will assume you wish to calculate your intake for two days.
